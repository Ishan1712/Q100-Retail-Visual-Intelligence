Use Your Own Real Photos

For the best test, take real photos of store shelves (DMart, Smart Bazaar, Reliance Fresh, etc.) with your phone and upload them. The AI works best with real photos.
  • Take a photo of a fully stocked biscuit/snack shelf → use as "matching" test
  • Take a photo of a different shelf (drinks, cereals) → use as "mismatch" test
  • Take a photo of a shelf with visible gaps/empty spots → use as "stock-out" test
